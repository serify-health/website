About Serify


Which clinics does Serify work with?

Serify works with most clinics in the United States. If you would like to check whether a clinic is supported by our service or if you would like to use our service with a clinic outside the United States, please send us an email at info@serify.org.

How much does it cost to be Serified?

Your first set of verifications will always be free. All subsequent verifications are also free for our beta users.

What happens if one or more of my STD test results is positive?

Serify only requests and verifies negative STD test results. Unverified and positive test results will not appear on your public profile and will automatically be flagged for your review.

I saw a user with a Serify negative STD status. Does this mean that the user does not have that STD?

No. Serify is a service that verifies medical information on file at reputable STD testing clinics. We do not assess whether this information is accurate or not. In addition, there is a possibility that a user with an STD may nonetheless test negative, or a user may contract an STD subsequent to a negative test. Please refer to the Terms of Use for additional information.

Whom can I contact for more information about Serify?

If you have any additional questions about our service, please send us an email at info@serify.org.

Who is your target audience?

Our target audience is dating app users who worry about STD infection even with consistent condom use and/or PrEP adherence. Serify aims to give these users a tool to further decrease their worry of infection.

What information do you collect?

We strive to collect the minimum amount of information possible. To verify your health information, we require your first name, last name, date of birth, clinic, and signature. You must also provide the specific negative test results with dates that you want verified. Please note that in order to protect your privacy, we do not collect or verify positive test results.

Are you HIPAA-compliant?

No, Serify is not a Covered Entity under HIPAA and therefore not subject to this federal regulation. That’s a good thing—we can help you share the test results that you want shared! Information that you choose not to share, for instance, your first name, last name, date of birth, clinic, and/or signature, will be stored securely according to industry standards for sensitive personal information. For more information on Covered Entity Guidance, please visit www.cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Administrative-Simplification/HIPAA-ACA/Downloads/CoveredEntitiesChart20160617.pdf.
